Treaty 5 Main Sponsour

NAN or Nishnawbe Aski Nation is our main sponsour at Treaty 5. NAN was developed in 1973 and was originally known as Grand Council Treaty No.9 until 1983. NAN represents 49 indigenous groups which is about 45,000 indigenous people related to treaty 9 and treaty 5. NAN strives for local self-determination while establishing spiritual, cultural, social, and economic independence.
